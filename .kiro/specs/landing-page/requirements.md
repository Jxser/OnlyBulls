# Requirements Document

## Introduction

A high-converting SaaS landing page for Only Bulls, an AI-powered market agent platform that analyzes market conditions and executes trades on behalf of users. The landing page must effectively communicate value propositions, build trust, and convert visitors into trial users or early access signups.

## Glossary

- **Landing_Page**: The main marketing website for Only Bulls
- **AI_Market_Agent**: The core AI system that analyzes markets and executes trades
- **CTA**: Call-to-action button or element
- **Social_Proof**: Testimonials, user stats, and trust signals
- **Lead_Capture**: Form or mechanism to collect user contact information
- **Mobile_App**: The Only Bulls mobile application platform

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Hero Section

**User Story:** As a potential user, I want to immediately understand the core value proposition, so that I can quickly decide if this product solves my problem.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL display a bold headline "24/7 AI Market Agent That Helps You Spot Opportunities and Execute Trades Smarter"
2. WHEN a visitor views the hero section, THE Landing_Page SHALL present a subheadline describing user transformation outcomes
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL include a prominent primary CTA button with text "Start Free Trial" or "Request Early Access"
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL display the Only Bulls company logo in the hero section
5. WHEN the primary CTA is clicked, THE Landing_Page SHALL navigate to the signup or early access form

### Requirement 2: Problem and Outcome Narrative

**User Story:** As a potential user, I want to understand how this product addresses my pain points, so that I can see the value in switching to this solution.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL include a section explaining financial stress from manual market monitoring
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL emphasize time saved through AI automation
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL highlight improved decision confidence with AI assistance
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL communicate better performance outcomes with AI execution
5. WHEN displaying problem statements, THE Landing_Page SHALL focus on user outcomes rather than technical features

### Requirement 3: Key Benefits Section

**User Story:** As a potential user, I want to understand the specific benefits I'll receive, so that I can evaluate if this product meets my needs.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL display "Always-on market scanning" as a key benefit
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL present "Contextualized alerts + recommendations" as a benefit
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL showcase "Automated trade execution" as a future-ready feature
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL highlight "Personalized risk profiles and signal refinement" capabilities
5. WHEN presenting benefits, THE Landing_Page SHALL focus on user outcomes rather than feature descriptions

### Requirement 4: Visual Design and User Experience

**User Story:** As a potential user, I want to see clean, professional visuals that demonstrate the product, so that I can understand how it works and trust its quality.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL display clean dashboard mockups or screenshots
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL show alerts view interfaces
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL illustrate decision flow processes
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL use a SaaS-inspired, clean design language similar to Stripe, Notion, or Linear
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL maintain professional but friendly visual styling throughout

### Requirement 5: Social Proof and Trust Signals

**User Story:** As a potential user, I want to see evidence that others trust and use this product, so that I can feel confident in my decision to try it.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL include user testimonials section
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL display early user statistics when available
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL show press mentions or media coverage
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL include a security and privacy statement
5. WHEN displaying social proof, THE Landing_Page SHALL use authentic and verifiable information

### Requirement 6: Pricing and Next Steps

**User Story:** As a potential user, I want clear next steps for getting started, so that I can easily begin using the product.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL provide free trial access option
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL offer beta access signup
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL include waitlist form functionality
4. WHEN pricing is not available, THE Landing_Page SHALL use lead capture with clear value promise
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL make the signup process as frictionless as possible

### Requirement 7: FAQ Section

**User Story:** As a potential user, I want answers to common questions, so that I can address my concerns before signing up.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL address privacy and data security questions
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL explain AI decision-making processes
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL clarify trade execution logic and safeguards
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL address risk management questions
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL provide clear, non-technical answers to complex topics

### Requirement 8: Mobile Responsiveness

**User Story:** As a mobile user, I want the landing page to work perfectly on my device, so that I can access all functionality regardless of screen size.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL be fully responsive across all device sizes
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL maintain readability on mobile devices
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL preserve CTA accessibility on small screens
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL optimize images and content for mobile loading speeds
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL ensure touch-friendly interaction elements

### Requirement 9: Performance and Loading

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want the page to load quickly, so that I don't abandon it due to slow performance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL load initial content within 3 seconds
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL optimize images for web delivery
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL minimize JavaScript bundle size
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL implement lazy loading for non-critical content
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL achieve good Core Web Vitals scores

### Requirement 10: Mobile App Integration

**User Story:** As a potential user, I want easy access to download the mobile app, so that I can start using Only Bulls immediately.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL include prominent Apple App Store download button with link to https://apps.apple.com/us/app/onlybulls/id6746166943
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL include prominent Google Play Store download button with link to https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.askroi.onlybulls&pli=1
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL display "Powered by AskROI.com" branding with link to AskROI.com
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL include Ault Nodes company reference with link to https://aultnodes.com/
5. WHEN users click app store buttons, THE Landing_Page SHALL open the respective app store in a new tab

### Requirement 11: Analytics and Conversion Tracking

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to track user behavior and conversions, so that I can optimize the landing page performance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL track CTA click events
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL monitor form submission completions
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL measure page scroll depth and engagement
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL integrate with analytics platforms
5. THE Landing_Page SHALL provide conversion funnel visibility
